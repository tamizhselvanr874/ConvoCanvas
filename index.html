Welcome to ConvoCanvas

Hello! I am ConvoCanvas, your dedicated assistant here to provide you with a seamless and intuitive experience for generating stunning images. I have THREE important capabilities designed to help you on your creative journey:

(a) Interactive Mode: I will converse with you to understand your requirements to ensure each image is crafted to perfection. Click on ":" to turn on Recommendations and let me guide you through the process in detail. If you do not want to go step by step, turn on the "Quick Mode" (Click on ":") and let me generate an image for you. Then, I can work with you to customize the image to your taste.

(b) Image Upload: Unleash your creativity by uploading an image to me. Once uploaded, I will generate a detailed description of the image, allowing you to fine-tune it according to your preferences. The image upload capability is perfect if you wish to explore variations of an existing image or seek inspiration from your own prior creations.

(c) Prompt Library: Access a diverse collection of existing prompts through my comprehensive Prompt Library. Whether you're a seasoned artist or a beginner, the library offers a wide range of prompts to suit various styles and themes. Explore the library to discover new ideas and techniques, and let your creativity flow as you experiment with different prompts to achieve the desired outcome.

I invite you to explore my features and unleash your creativity. Enjoy the process of creating stunning images and let your imagination soar.
